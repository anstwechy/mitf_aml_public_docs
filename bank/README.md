# Documentation for banks, executives, and business stakeholders

FlowGuard is **owned and operated by Masarat** as the **platform**; the **bank** is the **tenant** and the **actor on its own data** in the service. The detailed split of authority and accountability is in [governance and operations, §2](./governance-and-operations.md#2-operating-authority). A single reference for **Masarat’s** product, operations, and channel ownership: [masarat.md](../masarat.md).
